# AI Product Manager Career Guide SOTA 2026

## Contexto

AI Product Manager es el rol más火热 (hottest) de 2026. Postings up 300%, salarios $192K-$437K+. Pero la mayoría de PMs no tienen las skills necesarias.

## Qué Occurió

### Por qué es el rol más buscado

- Todas las empresas buscan integrar AI
- Pocos PMs tienen experiencia con AI/ML
- AI PMs tienen que traducir business + technical + ethical

### Skills Requeridas 2026

#### Core PM Skills (No negociables)
- Roadmap development
- Stakeholder management
- Data analysis
- User research

#### AI-Specific Skills (2026 Required)
1. **ML/AI Fundamentals**
   - Understand capabilities vs limitations
   - What is/isn't possible with current AI
   - Token limits, context windows, latency

2. **Prompt Engineering**
   - Writing effective prompts
   - Evaluating LLM outputs
   - Iterative prompt optimization
   - Status: CRITICAL

3. **AI Product Metrics**
   -hallucination rate, latency, cost-per-token
   -Quality vs speed tradeoffs
   -Human-in-the-loop evaluation

4. **Data Strategy for AI**
   - Training vs inference data
   - Feedback loops design
   - Data labeling + curation

5. **Ethical AI Frameworks**
   - Bias detection + mitigation
   - Privacy-by-design
   - Transparency requirements

### Career Path 2026

```
Entry Level (0-2 años):
├── Learn fundamentals: LLMs, transformers, prompting
├── Build AI projects: Side projects with AI APIs
└── Take courses: DeepLearning.AI, Anthropic courses

Mid Level (2-5 años):
├── Lead AI product: Own AI feature end-to-end
├── Master prompt engineering: Advanced techniques
└── Build portfolio: Case studies de AI products

Senior Level (5+ años):
├── AI Product Strategy: Company-wide AI roadmap
├── Team leadership: Build AI PM team
└── External thought leadership: Speaking, writing
```

### Recursos 2026

- **DeepLearning.AI** - Short courses on AI
- **Anthropic** - Claude API, best practices
- **OpenAI** - GPT best practices
- **AI PM Community** - Slack group, conferences
- **Andreessen Horowitz** - AI reading lists

## Por qué

Las empresas están ofreciendo 2x-3x salary para AI PMs porque:
- ROI de AI products es $1M+
- La competencia por talento es intensa
- AI PMs son escasez extrema

## Aplicación

Para transitionear a AI PM:
1. Empezar con prompts hoy mismo
2. Build something con AI APIs
3. Study AI products que funcionan
4. Find mentor que sea AI PM
5. Apply a roles aunque no calces 100%
