# Product Management SOTA Frameworks 2026

## Contexto

Product Management en 2026 requiere integrar AI, data-driven decisions, y discovery continuo. Los PMs necesitan equilibrar velocidad con calidad de decisión.

## Qué Occurió

### Frameworks Principales 2026

1. **Continuous Discovery + OKRs**
   - Discovery continuo integrado con OKRs trimestrales
   - Weekly customer interviews + metrics review
   - Status: SOTA

2. **Product-Led Growth (PLG) Framework**
   - Product como principal driver de adquisición
   - Free trial, freemium, self-serve onboarding
   - Status: SOTA

3. **HEART Metrics Framework (Google)**
   - Happiness, Engagement, Adoption, Retention, Task Success
   - Metrics hierarchy para productos
   - Status: SOTA

4. **AARRR Pirate Metrics**
   - Acquisition, Activation, Retention, Referral, Revenue
   - Funnel analysis completo
   - Status: Established

5. **RICE Scoring (Prioritization)**
   - Reach, Impact, Confidence, Effort
   - Quantitative prioritization
   - Status: Widely Adopted

6. **Jobs-to-Be-Done (JTBD)**
   - Focus en el "job" que el usuario contrata el producto
   - Outcome-based definition
   - Status: SOTA

### AI Tools for PMs 2026

- **Productboard** - AI-assisted insight clustering
- **Pendo** - In-app analytics + AI insights
- **Amplitude** - Product analytics + AI suggestions
- **Notion AI** - PRD generation, competitor analysis

## Por qué

PMs que usan data-driven frameworks:
- 3x más feature adoption
- 50% faster time-to-market
- Better stakeholder alignment

## Aplicación

```
PdM Flow 2026:
├── Discovery (Continuous)
│   ├── Customer Interviews (Weekly)
│   ├── Data Analysis (Continuous)
│   └── Competitor Research
├── Strategy (Quarterly)
│   ├── OKR Setting
│   └── Roadmap Prioritization (RICE)
├── Execution (Sprint-based)
│   ├── PRD Creation
│   ├── Acceptance Criteria
│   └── User Story Definition
└── Measure (Continuous)
    ├── HEART Metrics
    └── AARRR Funnel
```
