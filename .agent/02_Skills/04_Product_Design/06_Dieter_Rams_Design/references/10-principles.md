# The 10 Principles of Dieter Rams

## 1. Good design is innovative
- Does not copy existing designs
- Explores new technology and materials

## 2. Good design makes a product useful
- Serves a purpose
- Not just decorative

## 3. Good design is aesthetic
- Pleasing to use
- Respects the user

## 4. Good design makes a product understandable
- Self-explanatory
- Clear structure

## 5. Good design is unobtrusive
- Neutral, not decorative
- Like a tool, not art

## 6. Good design is honest
- Does not pretend to be something it's not
- Does not manipulate the user

## 7. Good design is long-lasting
- Avoids fashion trends
- Timeless, not trendy

## 8. Good design is thorough down to the last detail
- Nothing arbitrary
- Care and accuracy

## 9. Good design is environmentally friendly
- Conserves resources
- Minimizes waste

## 10. Good design is as little design as possible
- **Less, but better**
- Focus on essentials
- Back to purity
