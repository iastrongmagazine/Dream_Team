# Interactive Questions for Brand Setup

## Round 1: Basics
1. What's your brand name?
2. What does your brand do in one sentence?
3. What's your tagline (if any)?

## Round 2: Audience
4. Who is your primary audience?
5. What problem do you solve for them?
6. What do they care about most?

## Round 3: Personality
7. If your brand was a person, describe them in 3 adjectives:
   - Example: "Professional, innovative, trustworthy"
8. What brands do you admire? Why?
9. What brands do you NOT want to be like?

## Round 4: Visual
10. Do you have existing brand colors?
11. Do you have a logo?
12. Any visual references you like?

## Round 5: Voice
14. Formal or casual tone?
15. Technical or accessible language?
16. Serious or playful personality?

## Round 7: Output
17. Where should I save the brand files?
18. Any specific format requirements?
