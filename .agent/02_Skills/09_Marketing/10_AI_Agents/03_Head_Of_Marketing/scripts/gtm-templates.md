# Go-to-Market Templates

## Product Launch GTM

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (4-6 weeks before)

**Activities**:
- Finalize positioning and messaging
- Create sales enablement materials
- Prepare launch content
- Train sales team
- Set up tracking and analytics

**Owner**: Product Marketing

### Phase 2: Launch (Week of launch)

**Activities**:
- Press release
- Social media campaign
- Email campaign to customers
- Blog post / announcement
- Webinar (optional)

**Owner**: Marketing + Product

### Phase 3: Post-Launch (4-6 weeks after)

**Activities**:
- Measure initial results
- Collect customer feedback
- Optimize campaigns
- Create case studies

**Owner**: Marketing

### GTM Checklist

```
Pre-Launch:
[ ] Messaging framework approved
[ ] Sales enablement complete
[ ] Content ready
[ ] Tracking set up
[ ] Sales trained

Launch:
[ ] Press release sent
[ ] Social campaign live
[ ] Email sequence running
[ ] Landing page live

Post-Launch:
[ ] Results measured
[ ] Feedback collected
[ ] Optimizations made
```

## Enterprise GTM

### Key Differences from SMB

- Longer sales cycles (6-12 months)
- Multiple stakeholders
- Custom pricing and terms
- Dedicated account management

### Strategy Components

1. **Account selection**: Target list with ICP fit
2. **Account intelligence**: Research each account
3. **Multi-channel approach**: LinkedIn + email + events
4. **Content for decision makers**: Different for CEO vs. user
5. **Sales partnership**: Marketing supports sales, not drives

### Timeline

- Month 1-2: Research and planning
- Month 3-4: Outreach and engagement
- Month 5-8: Evaluation and negotiation
- Month 9+: Implementation

## Channel Partner GTM

### For SaaS with partners

1. **Partner program setup**:
   - Define tiers and benefits
   - Create enablement materials
   - Set up partner portal

2. **Partner recruitment**:
   - Identify potential partners
   - Outreach and recruitment
   - Agreement and onboarding

3. **Co-marketing**:
   - Joint content creation
   - Shared events
   - Co-branded materials
