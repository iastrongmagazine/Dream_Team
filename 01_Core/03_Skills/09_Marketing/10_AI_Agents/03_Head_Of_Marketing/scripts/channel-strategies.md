# Channel Strategies

## Paid Channels

### Google Ads

**Best for**: B2B, high-intent searches, measurable ROI

**Strategy**:
- Brand terms: Defend
- Competitor terms: Attack (if allowed)
- Category terms: Capture
- Long-tail: Scale

**Key Metrics**: CPC, CTR, Quality Score, Conversion Rate, ROAS

**Budget allocation**: Typically 20-30% of paid budget

### Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)

**Best for**: B2C, awareness, lookalike audiences

**Strategy**:
- Retargeting: High intent, convert
- Lookalikes: Scale new customers
- Broad: For awareness (with optimization)

**Key Metrics**: CPM, CPC, CTR, Cost per Result, ROAS

**Budget allocation**: Typically 20-30% of paid budget

### LinkedIn Ads

**Best for**: B2B, decision makers, ABM

**Strategy**:
- Account-based targeting
- Sponsored content for thought leadership
- InMail for outreach (expensive but targeted)

**Key Metrics**: CPL, MQL rate, Engagement rate

**Budget allocation**: 15-25% of paid budget

## Organic Channels

### SEO

**Best for**: Long-term growth, compound returns

**Strategy**:
- Technical SEO: Foundation
- Content: Pillar pages + cluster content
- Link building: Earned, not bought

**Key Metrics**: Organic traffic, keyword rankings, domain authority

**Timeline**: 6-12 months for significant results

### Content Marketing

**Best for**: Thought leadership, inbound, SEO

**Strategy**:
- Blog posts: Educational, SEO-focused
- Case studies: Social proof
- Whitepapers: Lead generation
- Podcasts/Webinars: Reach

**Key Metrics**: Traffic, engagement, leads generated

### Social Media

**Best for**: Brand building, community, engagement

**Strategy**:
- LinkedIn: B2B thought leadership
- Twitter/X: Industry conversation
- YouTube: Tutorial, product demos

**Key Metrics**: Followers, engagement rate, reach
